AskGeorge+ is an AI-powered chatbot developed for George Brown College. The chatbot leverages natural language processing and embeddings to intelligently understand and answer student-related queries.

## Key Technical Features
- Built with **Python** and standard NLP libraries.
- Uses modern embedding models to retrieve the right context for incoming questions.
- Automatically resolves internal college administrative questions without human intervention.
